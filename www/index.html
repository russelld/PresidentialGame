INSTRUCTIONS

Follow these steps to record your electoral votes and the week of play:

At the end of each turn click on the state(s) your team has won.

Click on a state 1x for Republican/Red, 2x for Democrat/Blue or 3x for Neutral/Tan.

At the top of the map the total number of electoral votes are shown for each team.

Use the arrows in the lower right to change the week counter at the end of the round.

Clicking “New Game” will reset both the map and the week counter.

Your iPad app will function and display best in Landscape mode.
